Reading Materials

The very first book that was assigned to me was Thinking With Type. I'll be honest, my typical process for selecting fonts has been selecting fonts that I thought look good visually. Ellen Lupton, the author of Thinking With Type has redefined my process and the way I think about fonts. It's simply not enough to pick a font that looks good, one must consider the audience, content, layout, and other factors before settling on a font. Part of the reading assigment required an accompanying typography site, showcasing what I learned, you can view that here.
