Simple Font Pairing Tips

Successfully pairing fonts can be an arduous task! Keeping in mind these few tips will help you get started.

The Skeleton

When considering the skeleton of the fonts, try to keep the width, x-height, and proportions similar.

The Meat

The meat of a font is in reference to it's weight. While it may not seem apparent due to the thicker shape, good pairs will share similar skeletons regardless of its meat.

The Contrast

A good way to provide contrast for your pairs, is choosing a sans-serif and serif font that share a similar skeleton with varying meat sizes.
